About Peekabox

Peekabox is a food-tech platform that helps bakeries, restaurants, and grocery stores sell surplus food at a discount. Customers get exclusive deals on surprise boxes, while businesses boost revenue and reduce waste.

Shaping the Future

Mission

At Peekabox, our mission is to reduce food waste by connecting bakeries, restaurants, and grocery stores with consumers looking for affordable, high-quality surplus food.

Vision

To create a world where food waste is minimized, and every surplus meal finds a home—empowering businesses, benefiting consumers, and contributing to a more sustainable future.

Why Partner with Peekabox?

Increase Revenue

Turn surplus food into extra income by selling it at a discount instead of wasting it. Peekabox helps you profit from unsold inventory.

Increase Customers

Attract new customers seeking affordable, high-quality meals. First-time buyers can turn into loyal patrons, helping to increase your regular sales.

Sustainable

Minimize food waste and support a more sustainable food system. Partnering with Peekabox helps your business meet sustainability goals and strengthen brand reputation.
